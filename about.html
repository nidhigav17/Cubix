Cubix Control Systems

Headquartered in Noida, Cubix Control Systems (P) Ltd. is an IATF 16949, ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ISO 45001 certified Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) company.

Cubix is committed to deliver high quality, innovative and cost effective end-to-end electronics solutions to valuable customers by leveraging its unparalleled manufacturing experience and state-of-the-art infrastructure.

The company offers full suite of services in avant-garde sectors such as consumer electronics, automotive, telecom, IT-peripherals, lighting, home appliances, defence and many more.

With strategic proximity to New-Delhi, Cubix has two manufacturing facilities located in NOIDA with total production area of 35,000 sq. ft.

Our History

At the root of our success lies the vision of an Entrepreneur.

Matching opportunity with core skill-set and an entrepreneurial vision. Late Shri R.K. Chaudhry established Cubix Control Systems (P) Ltd. with a humble beginning in Year 1989.

Over the last 30 Years, Cubix has become a leading Electronics Manufacturing Services provider and is synonymous with excellence and expertise in Electronics industry.With approximately 400+ employees, Cubix has an unrelenting focus on quality, delivery, value, and technology in everything we make and do.

Corporate Social

Responsibility

We want our impact to reverberate throughout the world and across communities. Therefore, we are committed to conducting our business in an ethical, socially responsible and environmentally sustainable manner. This commitment is consistent with our corporate objectives and is essential to continued business success.

Occupational Health & Safety

We are committed to providing healthy, safe and ergonomic work environment and risk-free processes that enable our employees to work injury and illness free. We consider it our responsibility to go beyond the workplace health and safety regulations and protect our employees from dangerous materials, noise, and unpleasant chemicals. We are OHSAS 18001:2007 certified for Occupational Health and Safety Management System.

Environmental Stability

Environmental Stability is an important goal for us. We evaluate the environmental impact of our industrial processes and work to identify ways to make them more sustainable. Specifically, we work to reduce our consumption of paper, electricity and production chemicals. We are ISO 14001:2004 certified for Environmental Management Systems
